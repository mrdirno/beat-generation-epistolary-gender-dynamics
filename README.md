## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a 'lost' letter from Carolyn Moriarty to Neal, responding to the affair from her perspective.
- Draft a journal entry from Neal, written in the truck heading east, that she would never send to Jack.
- Compose a monologue from Dean, hustling pool in Denver, explaining his side of the 'metaphysics' to a stranger.
- Write the scene where Jack finally shows Neal what he's been writing about her and Dean.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive map tracing the journey of the letters (NYC, SF, Denver) with annotated excerpts.
- Create a text analysis tool comparing the linguistic 'fingerprints' of Jack's narration vs. Neal's quoted letters.
